Introduction

This was a 20 week project for my minor. My minor was focussed on designing innovation for a group of a billion people. This meant I had to think in the context of a lot of different cultures. I’ve decided to focus on saving money. Specifically on the person that experiences procrastination while saving for a specific goal.

Research

For my research I used a lot of creative methods. The first iteration I wanted to know how people spend their money. Thus I’ve used this format to test several people. In this format the vertical line stands for buying intensity, the horizontal line for how much value they give the type of product, the yellow line for what they desire. Then a smiley for the emotion.
