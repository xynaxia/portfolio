Introduction

I did this project for my minor. It was a 20 week project, with international students. The minor focussed on Innovation Design, that might be relevant for a billion people. Therefore, the design must work for different all different cultures. After a lot of brainstorming and getting concepts, eventually I choose to design something to help a person saving money. Since in different cultures the value of saving money is very different, it was quite a difficult thing to do.
