More about me...

Greetings, I’m Dario. I define myself as a Digital Product Designer, not as a UI designer. Because at the core I try to solve problems humans face with technology in mind, but the specific technology used is not a means to an end.


As a designer I differentiate myself as a social designer. In my process I’m highly social and attempt to use the collective knowledge of people around me to create the best products. Whether this is testing with users, collaborating with fellow designers or discussing ideas with developers.
