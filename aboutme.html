More about me...

Greetings, I’m Dario. I’m a UX Designer with a focus on qualitative UX research for Digital Products. I like to go through the whole design cycle, however discovery is what I most enjoy doing. I have a passion for products that touch both the physical and the digital, such as AR, or the UI of a self-driving car.

Mission

My mission as a designer to the world is helping create digital products that help humans reach their goals. Whether that is helping people to eat more healthily, or help make someone's job a little easier by taking out the boring parts.

My Process

The core of my process is that of Design Thinking. Where I most differentiate myself is that I am a very social designer. Beside seeking out users, I pro-actively set up networks of different experts and stakeholders, to use their collective knowledge to power my own design.
