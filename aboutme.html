More about me...

Greetings, I’m Dario. I’m a Digital Product Designer. At the core I try to solve problems humans face with technology in mind, but the specific technology used is not a means to an end.


As a designer I differentiate myself as a social designer. In my process I attempt to use the collective knowledge of people around me to create the best products. Whether this is testing with users, collaborating with fellow designers or discussing ideas with developers.
